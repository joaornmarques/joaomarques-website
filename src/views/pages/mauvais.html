Mauvais Sang

Motion Design

April 2016

Academic work (FCT University of Coimbra)

Visual score of an excert of the film Mauvais Sang, from Leos Carax, which include the songs J'ai pas d'regrets by Serge Reggiani and Modern Love by David Bowie. The challange purposed on this project was to transform all the sound present on this excert into visual elements, so it was taken as base a few elementar geometric shapes to represent specific sounds and musical instruments, with the differentiation between musical and silent scenes maded by the contrast between black and white.
