ENEMM'19

Branding & Graphic design

March 2019

NEI/AAC

The multimedia students national meeting (ENEMM) 4th edition had place in Coimbra, Portugal. An event full of talks, workshops and exhibitions approaching the different areas of multimedia, like AR/VR, videmapping, web design and many others. I had been invited to do the branding of this edition, in collaboration with Rúben Silva and Sofia Torres. The core element explored was a modular grid, inspired on multimedia's tools and objects such as electric boards or led monitors. The color pallete was a reference to the host city official colors, yellow and purple.

Four days of talks, workshops and exhibitions in the most varied areas of multimedia.
