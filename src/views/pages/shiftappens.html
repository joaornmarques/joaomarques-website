Shift APPens'16

Branding & Graphic design

February 2016

NEI/AAC, JeKnowledge

Shift APPens is a hackathon that occours annualy since 2014 in Coimbra, Portugal. The branding of the third edition was maded by me in a collaboration with Adriana Nunes. The motto of this hackathon is to be the most random of all, with funny and unexpected events happening during the competition. To reflect that spirit, it was chosen four bright and strong colors for the base of the identity.

Divulgation material
— T-shirts, Posters, ID tags
